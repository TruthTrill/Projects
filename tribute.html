Info About truth Trill:

"A real rapper trying to escape the perils of life through music."

Truth Trill, real name Charles Barnes. Considered by some as "The Greatest Lyricist of the 21st Century" and founder of the enigmatic Celestia Digital Corp.

 

    A renowned lyrical artist and self proclaimed sage, operative and operations commander. He founded the Lyrical Forces, the Androcity Artist Community, Shadowseen Graphic Designs and Celestia Digital Music Group, in order to realize his ambitions of creating a nation for elite artist. He earned such monikers as "the Legendary Master" and "Truth the Disaster," feared in lyricism and in general by both friend and foe as a genius and a madman. Possessing an IQ of 180 and fluent in the language of the streets. He was known as "the Man Who Makes the Impossible Possible" and his exploits made him into a living legend among the underground whilst having nearly nothing. Truth Trill was later inducted into the "Philosophers" group of lyrical masters and poets and uses his influence to further his own ambitions, the "Celestial Wisemen" and other agendas. Currently Truth Trill is working on the Thrice Great Trilogy. Showcasing his Past, Present and Future Skill.
